Pre-NCM

Sendai symposium on the eye, head and body movement control systems, a special event of Annual Meeting of Neural Control of Movement

Date：April 17 (Fri), 2026
Organizer：Mayu Takahashi (Tohoku University, Japan)

About Pre-NCM


In 2026, NCM will be held in Kobe, Japan. Through this opportunity, we would like to gather leading scientists and their trainees from all-over the world to discuss their outstanding works with Japanese neuroscientists. Simultaneously, we would like to introduce the Japanese culture, especially the charm points in Sendai, to them.
