Pre-NCM

Sendai symposium on the eye, hand and head movement control systems, a satellite event of Annual Meeting of Neural Control of Movement

Date：April 17 (Fri), 2026
Organizer：Mayu Takahashi(Univ. Tohoku, Japan)

About Pre-NCM

This is an official satellite event of the NCM meeting, which started in 1991 and has been bring together scientists seeking to understand the neural mechanisms that allow the activities of daily life, through the brain’s control of the eyes, head, trunk, and limbs.

In 2026, the annual meeting will be held in Kobe, Japan. Through this opportunity, we would like to gather leading scientists and their trainees from all-over the world to discuss their outstanding works with Japanese neuroscientist. Simultaneously, we would like to introduce the Japanese culture, especially the charm points in Sendai to them.
